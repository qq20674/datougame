About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival game where you must battle against endless hordes of monsters in a constantly evolving world. As you progress, you'll unlock new abilities, weapons, and power-ups to help you survive longer against increasingly difficult waves of enemies.

The game features beautiful pixel art graphics, an immersive soundtrack, and addictive gameplay that will keep you coming back for more. Can you beat your high score and become the ultimate survivor?

How to Play

Controls

  • WASD Move your character
  • Mouse Aim your weapons
  • Space Dodge roll (if available)
  • 1-4 Use special abilities

Tips for Beginners

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters
  • Collect experience orbs to level up faster
  • Prioritize health upgrades early in the game
  • Focus on a few strong weapons rather than many weak ones
  • Use environmental obstacles to your advantage
